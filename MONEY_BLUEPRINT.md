# The Money Blueprint: A Plain-English Guide for the Indian Retail Investor (2026)

*I'm a financial educator, not a SEBI-registered investment advisor. Everything below is education, not personalised advice. Fund names are concrete examples to make this real — verify every one yourself before you put a rupee in.*

The big idea in one breath: **First fill the guaranteed buckets (EPF/VPF at 8.25% tax-free, then PPF). Then build a growth core of low-cost Indian index + factor funds, add some global, sprinkle gold + REITs, and keep cash in arbitrage/liquid funds.** Boring on purpose. Boring compounds.

---

## 1. The Allocation

### Tier 0 — Guaranteed buckets (do these FIRST)

| Bucket | How much | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **EPF + VPF** | Up to ₹2.5 lakh/yr combined employee contribution | 8.25% tax-free, rock-safe. Tell HR to start VPF. Interest above ₹2.5L/yr becomes taxable — size near that cap. |
| **PPF** | ₹1.5 lakh/yr | EEE (tax-free in, growth, and out). The bedrock. Deposit before the 5th of the month. |

### Tier 1 — The growth core (your investable surplus)

| Bucket | % | Specific fund(s) |
|---|---|---|
| **Indian broad-market core** | 35% | Motilal Oswal Nifty 500 Index – Direct Growth (large+mid+small in one). Alt: Navi Nifty 50 Index – Direct (lowest cost, large-cap only). |
| **Indian multi-factor (quality + low-vol tilt)** | 25% | Edelweiss Nifty500 Multicap Momentum Quality 50 – Direct + Nippon India Nifty Alpha Low Volatility 30 – Direct (split ~half each). |
| **Global / US equity** | 20% | Navi or Kotak Nasdaq 100 FoF – Direct; add Motilal Oswal S&P 500 – Direct *if open*. (LRS via INDmoney/Vested if funds capped.) |
| **Gold** | 5% | Nippon India Gold Savings Fund – Direct (FoF, SIP-able, no demat). Demat: Zerodha/ICICI Gold ETF. |
| **REIT / InvIT** | 5% | Embassy Office Parks REIT + IndiGrid InvIT (need demat). |
| **Cash / parking** | 10% | Tata Arbitrage Fund – Direct (3+ month money) + Axis Liquid Fund – Direct (emergency). |

> Rule of thumb: Indian equity 60% · global 20% · gold+REITs 10% · cash 10%. Tilt more to cash if nervous or saving for a near-term goal.

## 3. How to Actually Start This Week

1. **Pick ONE platform.** Simplest, no demat → **Groww**. Want everything in a demat → **Zerodha Coin**. Both free, both sell **direct** plans. (Demat needed separately only for REITs/InvITs/ETFs.)
2. **Finish KYC once** (PAN + Aadhaar e-KYC, ~10 min).
3. **Start VPF + PPF before SIPs.** Email HR for extra VPF (target EPF+VPF ≈ ₹2.5L/yr). Open PPF via net-banking, standing instruction before the 5th.
4. **Create the AutoPay mandate FIRST.** Coin: Account → Mandates → Create → Confirm Autopay → UPI PIN (₹1 test debit auto-refunded). UPI Autopay for small SIPs; eNACH for large.
5. **Set each SIP.** Pick fund → **confirm it says DIRECT, not Regular** → Buy → mandate → amount, Monthly, **date 2–3 days after salary** → Create SIP.
6. **Verify global-fund status** — Nasdaq/S&P funds get capped by SEBI's overseas limit; check the AMC site says *open* before that SIP.
7. **Register on MF Central** (mfcentral.com) — one consolidated statement + capital-gains report for tax.
8. **Walk away.** A failed UPI installment carries no penalty and doesn't cancel future ones. Just keep the bank funded on SIP date.

| Asset | STCG | LTCG | LT after |
|---|---|---|---|
| Indian equity / equity index / arbitrage | 20% | 12.5% above ₹1.25L/yr | 12 mo |

| REIT / InvIT | 20% | 12.5% | 12 mo |
| EPF/VPF (≤₹2.5L/yr) & PPF | Tax-free | Tax-free | — |

*Add 4% cess. Each SIP installment has its own holding-period clock.*

---

## 5. The 3 Golden Rules

1. **Automate everything.** Mandate + date after payday. The investor who does nothing usually beats the one who fiddles.
2. **Never panic-sell.** Factor and global funds *will* lag for years — normal, not a defect. Hold 7–10 years.
3. **Never pay for tips.** No "guaranteed return" calls, no tipsters, no Regular plans pushed by a bank RM (~0.5–1%/yr commission quietly eats lakhs). Direct plans, index funds, done.
